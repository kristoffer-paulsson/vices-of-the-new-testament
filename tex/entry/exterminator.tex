\item[Exterminator,]
\entlbl{exterminator}

\grc{διώκτης}
\index[grc]{διωκτης@\grc{διώκτης}}
(\textit{diōktēs}):
All Liddell, Thayer, and Gingrich agree on \emph{persecutor} which defines as ``someone who treats a particular group of people cruelly.''\cdfoot{persecutor}{2023-03-14} Also, \emph{exterminator} as ``a person whose job is to kill a particular type of animal that is not wanted in a place,''\cdfoot{exterminator}{2023-03-14} where the Christians are considered unwanted pestilence.
Found in 1~Tim 1:13.
