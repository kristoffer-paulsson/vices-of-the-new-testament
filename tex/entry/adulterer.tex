\item[Adulterer,]
\entlbl{adulterer}

\grc{μοιχός}
\index[grc]{μοιχος@\grc{μοιχός}}
(\textit{moichos}):
In the Bible, according to BNM, both occasions in given lists are in \emph{masculine plural}\footnote{1 Cor 6:9; word ``17. \grc{μοιχοί μοιχός} - nnmp (noun nominative masculine plural),'' Heb 13:4; word ``13. \grc{μοιχούς μοιχός} - namp (noun accusative masculine plural),'' (BNM) \emph{BibleWorks 10.0.8.755} BibleWorks, 2017.}, which means it is only male adulterers that God actively deals with when they commit adultery, no matter Jewish or Christian men, by judging them or exclude them from heaven. 
Found in 1~Cor 6:9; Heb 13:4.
