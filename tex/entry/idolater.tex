\item[Idolater,]
\entlbl{idolater} 

\grc{εἰδωλολάτρης}
\index[grc]{ειδωλολατρης@\grc{εἰδωλολάτρης}} 
(\textit{eidōlolatrēs}):
According to Liddell ``\emph{an idol-worshipper}, \emph{idolater}.** The sinners cataloged in Eph 5:5 -- \grc{πόρνος} (\emph{pornos}) i. \entref{male-sex-buyer} ii. \entref{male-prostitute}, \grc{ἀκάθαρτος} (\emph{akathartos}) \entref{depravement}, \grc{πλεονέκτης} (\emph{pleonektēs}) \entref{unscrupulous competition} -- counts as idolaters. Also, see \entref{idolatry}, \entref{idol meat}, \entref{idol pollution}, and \entref{idol worship}.
Found in Eph 5:5; 1~Cor 5:10, 11, 6:9; Rev 21:8, 22:15.
