\item[Betrayer,]
\entlbl{betrayer}

\grc{προδότης}
\index[grc]{προδοτης@\grc{προδότης}}
(\textit{prodotēs}):
All Liddell, Thayer, and Gingrich mention \emph{betrayer} together with \emph{traitor}. \emph{Betrayer} is "a person who is not loyal to their country or to another person, often doing something harmful such as giving information to an enemy."\cdfoot{betrayer}{2023-04-01}
Found in 2~Tim 3:4.
