Liddell describes ``[1.] \emph{man-slaying} \ldots [2.] of women, \emph{murdering husbands}.'' Manslayer is a crime of the worst sort. May the person doing this --~if a woman~-- repent, and may her children forgive her.
Found in 1~Tim 1:9.
